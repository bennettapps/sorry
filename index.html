I'm So Sorry

I had one job, that's right?

How do I say this, without a fight...

A Christmas gift, I failed to procure,

Ashamed and remorseful, I endure.

Yet in my forgetfulness, I'll make it right,

For tomorrow, dance lessons will be in sight.

But alas, there's nothing at your door today,

I apologize for the delay.

This site, beyond an apology, I declare,

A promise to improve, with utmost care.

Next time I forget something, oh so dumb,

An anniversary, perhaps, I'll succumb.

All you have to do is pull up this site,

And I'll repent, make everything right.

- I am Jesse
